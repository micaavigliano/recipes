- 1 egg
- 120gr brown sugar
- 60gr mealted butter
- 150gr integral flour
- 1 1/2 oat flakes
- 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
- 2 teaspoons of peanut butter
- chocolate chips (optional)
- vanilla extract a little bit

1. Preheat the oven to 180ºC
2. Mix the brown sugar with the egg
3. Once the brown sugar and the egg are well mixed, add the melted butter and mix again
4. Add the vanilla extract and the peanut butter and mix again
5. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, the oat flakes and the baking powder
6. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until you have a homogeneous dough
7. If you want to add chocolate chips, add them now and mix again
8. With the help of a spoon, make small balls with the dough and place them on
9. Bake for 15 minutes. Generally, I put the 15 minutes timer and then I turn off the oven and let the cookies in there for another 5 minutes, so they are a little bit more crunchy. But you can skip this step if you like your cookies softer.

Enjoy!!
