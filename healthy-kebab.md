- 500 gr of meat (I used a mix of cow and pork meat, not halal but I am not muslim :P)
- seasoning: paprika, comino, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, parsley, and if you like peperoncino
- greek yogurt 100 gr
- 1 green/red/yellow bell pepper
- 1 onion
- a wrapper of your preference (I used the flour tortilla, but you can replace it with whatever you like: lettuce-?-, corn tortilla, a piadina, bread, etc)
- salad
- baking paper
- sriracha (optional)
- pickles (optional)

1. preheat the oven at 200 degrees
2. season the meat with the seasoning
3. add the greek yogurt to the mix
4. mix everything
5. cut the veggies in big squares
6. place portions of the meat onto a sheet of baking paper and spread it as thin and even as possible, then roll it tightly to form a compact log shape
7. once the meat rolls are done, put them in the oven along with the veggies
8. let them cook for 20 minutes
9. unwrap the meat and slice the rolls thinly
10. and done! now enjoy your kebab sandwich with the salad and the toppings
